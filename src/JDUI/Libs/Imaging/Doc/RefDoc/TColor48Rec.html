TColor48Rec Record
ImagingTypes

Color record for 48 bit images, which allows access to individual color channels.

Pascal
public TColor48Rec = packed record
  case LongInt of
    0: (B: Word;
        G: Word;
        R: Word;);
    1: (Channels: array[0..2] of Word););
  end;
